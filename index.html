// What We Are Asking For

Our Demands

1
End the Flock Contracts
Nevada County SO, Grass Valley PD, and Nevada City PD should terminate their contracts with Flock Safety. Over 30 communities — including Austin, Denver, Cambridge, and Santa Cruz — have already done so.
2
Explain the Stripped Records
Nearly 7 million searches with every reason column removed from the public records response. Flock's own portal says reasons are stored indefinitely. The public has a right to know why they were hidden.
3
Answer for the Federal Access
ATF, FBI, Postal Inspection Service, Air Force bases, NCIS, and a federal fusion center all searched Nevada County residents' plates. Under what authority? Residents deserve answers.
4
Full AB 481 Compliance Review
California law requires Board of Supervisors approval before deploying surveillance technology. Was the Board ever told about the true scope of this network? A proper public hearing is required by law.
